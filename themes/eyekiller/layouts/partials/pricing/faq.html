Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Desktop and Web based?

With both options, your data is stored securely on {{ if eq .Site.LanguageCode "en-GB" }}TM3 owned{{else}}Microsoft Azure{{end}} servers in {{ i18n "location" }}. The difference is how you access it. With Desktop, the program is installed on your Windows PC or laptop. With web based, nothing is installed on your machines; you access the program through your web browser.

Do I need/can I have both Desktop and Web versions?

It depends what you need for your clinic. You can have both, or use just one. By understanding your needs, we will determine which is the best solution for you. There is no difference in price.

How does licensing work?

TM3 licensing is based on the number of practitioner diaries required. Other users such as receptionists, administrators, book keepers and practice managers can use TM3 at no additional cost.

Can I use TM3 on a Mac?

Yes. TM3 works seamlessly on MacBooks, iPads and iMacs. From booking appointments to using clinical notes, TM3 excels on both Apple and Windows products.

What is my initial contract length?

TM3 contracts last 12 months. After the initial contract period it will become a rolling contract, however, so you can cancel with one month's notice.

I am using a system already. Can I bring my patient data with me?

Yes! TM3 has a dedicated team that migrates patient data from other systems on a day to day basis. Call us to discuss your options.
