30 reasons to move from TM2 to TM3

Top 5

  • Flexibility - Access your diary from anywhere
  • Access TM3 on multiple devices, including iPad, iMac, tablets etc.
  • No cost for administration staff
  • TM3 Sketchpad - get creative with freehand drawing and annotations
  • New HTML templates with beautiful fonts and a lot more functionality around adding pictures, logos and designing company signatures.

Communications

  • Location specific confirmation emails
  • 3 different SMS delivery options - you can now personalise who the messages are sent from
  • Improved email design and functionality, including new fonts and branding opportunities.
  • Drag and drop documents onto TM3 for quicky and easy uploading.
  • Easily email GP/medical contacts a personalised letter with just a few simple clicks

Clinical

  • Scores and goals - create, set and record scores and goals with your patient
  • Fully customisable flags and alerts to suit clinic's circumstances
  • Spell check via your browser
  • TM3 Sketchpad - get creative with freehand drawing and annotations
  • Previous medical history follows a patient for all cases during their time in your clinic
  • Prepoluated medication and vaccinations are listed for you and can be easily customised to suit the way you practice.
  • Highlight/summary section of your individual clinical cases
  • Greater clinical governance

Flexibility

  • Access your diary from anywhere
  • Access TM3 on multiple devices
  • Unlimited concurrent logins

Security

  • Improved security auditing
  • Timed automatic logout

Support and training

  • Live Chat available within TM3
  • Knowledge base with how-to videos

Administration

  • Timed and recurring interactive tasks - add a time to your tasks and assign to different users
  • Global Client search (quickly find patient from diary)
  • Quick and easy appointment summary in the patient record

Clinic Insight

  • Gain a snapshot of your clinic activity over the previous 30 days from the dashboard
  • Easy export reports (complete with formatting)
  • Clever report filtering - split data or remove columns, how you filter your data is up to you

Diary

  • Block book events as well as appointments
  • The 'do next screen' - ensures there is always a follow-up to when a patient leaves their session
  • All your 'to-dos' are listed conveniently on your dashboard to help keep you organised
  • Diary summary button (quick update as to how the day is going)

Finance

  • Email batch invoices quickly and easily
  • Paperless invoicing (email Invoice after each appointment)
  • Redesigned invoice wizard for simplified and more effective invoicing
  • Improved authorisation management - manage authorisation codes from the diary and clinical notes
